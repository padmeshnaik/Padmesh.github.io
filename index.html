Publications and Research Work

Image Forgery: Detection of Manipulated Images Using Neural Network

Published : 2020

Conference: International Conference on Recent Advances in Computational Techniques (IC-RACT)

Authors: Nitin Kumar, Padmesh Naik, Nikhil Raina, Deepali Kayande
Affiliation: Department of Computer Engineering, D.B.I.T, Mumbai, India

Paper Link : https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3682481

Abstract: This paper introduces a novel image manipulation detection system utilizing metadata analysis and Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN). It emphasizes the significance of digital forensics in the era of easily manipulated digital media, presenting a two-pronged approach for identifying image forgeries. The study highlights the use of Error Level Analysis for detecting compression inconsistencies and proposes a CNN model trained to discern manipulated images, aiming at enhancing digital image authenticity and integrity verification.

Keywords: Image forensics, Metadata Analysis, Error Level Analysis, Convolutional Neural Network, Neural network
